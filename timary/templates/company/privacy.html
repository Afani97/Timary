A Privacy page built for Humans

The following sections outline the different kinds of data we collect and how we use them.

Your Email

Our payment provider Stripe requires that you provide an email while purchasing our services. This data is not shared with third parties.

Your Name

Our payment provider Stripe requires that you provide a name while purchasing our services. This data is not shared with third parties.

Your Phone Number

We use Twilio as our sms provider to send texts. This data is not shared with third parties.

Your Debit Card

Our payment provider Stripe requires that you provide a name debit card purchasing our services. We do not log any ip address or store any ip address in our database. This data is not shared with third parties and is never accessed by our application.

Your IP Address

Stripe requires that we provide your ip address when registering for Timary. We do not log any ip address or store any ip address in our database. This data is not shared with third parties and is never accessed by our application.

That's about it.

If you have any further concerns, feel free to talk to us through email.
