FAQ

Do you have a mobile app?

Not yet, but something is in the works.

How long is the free trial?

30 days when you register. If you're card is not successfully charged after the 30 days, your account will be unable to accept payments and sync your accounting.

Which accounting software do you currently support?

Quickbooks, Freshbooks, Zoho, Sage and Xero. If you use another platform, let us know and we can add it to the roadmap.

How does the referral discounts work?

You can have a maximum of two referral discounts applied to your account, $5 each. You may refer more if you like, but the maximum is $10, forever while you keep your subscription active.

What type of invoices do you currently support?

At the moment, recurring invoices on an interval of your choosing. Milestones so an invoice is split by when you decide to send it off. And weekly, a flat-rate fee if hourly doesn't apply to you. We do have one-off invoices but as a free tool outside of Timary.

Do you support a one-off invoices in Timary?

Yes indeed, support for allowing clients to pay in installments or have it recurring happening soon.

What kinds of payments can my clients use to pay me with?

If you're in the U.S., a typical credit/debit card or bank info from all major banks are supported by Stripe.

When can I expect the funds in my bank account?

That depends on Stripe, but we hope to cut it down to a couple of days when your client pays the invoice.

Will you support creating 1099 IRS tax forms?

It's on the list! That way you don't need to connect your accounting software if you don't have one.
